Smart Savings

SmartSavings™ gives you the opportunity to invest your money at high rates with low costs. We give you the choice of picking one, or several, of the investment vehicles of your preference. You can look for new investment opportunities, invest, withdraw and follow the gains all from the application. Start investing with as low as $10!
